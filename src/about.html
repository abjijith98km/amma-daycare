Established in 2012, Amma Kindergarten has been dedicated to providing exceptional childcare and educational services to children and families in our community. With a rich history spanning over a decade, we have earned a reputation for excellence in early childhood education and nurturing development in a warm and welcoming environment.

Our Philosophy:
At Amma Kindergarten, we believe that every child is unique and deserves the opportunity to learn and grow in a supportive and stimulating environment. We are committed to fostering a love for learning, encouraging creativity, and promoting social-emotional development in all aspects of our programs.

Our Programs:
We offer a range of programs tailored to meet the diverse needs of children at different stages of development:
Kindergarten: Our kindergarten program provides a solid foundation for academic success, focusing on early literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking skills.
Daycare: Our daycare services offer a safe and nurturing environment for children to thrive while parents are at work or away.
After-School Program: Our after-school program provides a structured yet fun-filled environment where children can engage in enriching activities and receive homework assistance.
Pre-School: Our pre-school program prepares children for kindergarten by fostering social skills, independence, and a love for learning through play-based activities.

Our Team:
Our team consists of passionate educators who are dedicated to providing high-quality care and education to every child. With years of experience and expertise in early childhood development, our staff members create a supportive and nurturing environment where children feel valued, respected, and encouraged to reach their full potential.

Our Facility:
Located in a safe and convenient location, our facility is designed to provide a welcoming and engaging space for children to learn and play. We maintain clean and well-equipped classrooms and learning resources to support children’s growth and development.

Join Us:
We welcome you to become a part of the Amma Kindergarten family! Whether you’re looking for childcare, preschool, kindergarten, or after-school services, we are here to support your child’s journey every step of the way.

Contact us today to learn more about our programs and how we can help your child thrive at Amma Kindergarten.
